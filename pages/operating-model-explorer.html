The bundle's operating model

Recipes install kinds — a domain vocabulary — and typed links weave relationships across them. Three ways to make that visible, rendered from this bundle's real conventions.

snapshot

Solution A — a dedicated graph. Kinds are nodes coloured by the recipe that installed them; edges are the typed-link vocabulary. The story it tells: Task (work-tracking) is the hub every other recipe points into. Hover a node for its fields and links.

Hover or tap a kind to inspect its fields and typed links.

Solution B — on the launcher landing. No new destination: a compact operating-model strip sits above the usual Dashboards/Documents on the ui home, so every session sees the shape of the bundle it's working in. (Mock of the launcher; the strip is the proposed addition.)
